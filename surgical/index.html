Cats

Surgical support for cats includes pre-op health assessments, careful anesthesia monitoring during surgery, and post-op pain management. Ensuring a safe recovery environment and proper care helps minimize risks and supports healing.

Dogs

Surgical support for dogs involves pre-op health checks, careful anesthesia management during the procedure, and post-op pain relief. Providing a calm recovery space and monitoring for complications ensures a safe and smooth healing process.

Other Pets

Surgical support for other pets, such as rabbits, guinea pigs, or birds, includes pre-operative health evaluations, appropriate anesthesia, and careful monitoring during surgery. Post-operative care focuses on pain management, wound care, and creating a stress-free environment to aid in recovery. Each species requires tailored care to ensure optimal healing and safety.
